How is this different from LinkedIn?+

LinkedIn is for careers. youbet is for finding the one person you build something with. We match on gaps, ambition and commitment — not job titles.

How long does it take to find a match?+

Usually 2–7 days. We don't rush it — a bad match is worse than no match. When we find someone who genuinely fits, we email and DM you immediately.

What makes the matching actually work?+

We ask what slows you down — your honest gap. That gap is exactly what your match is great at. Skills + gaps + commitment + location. Better signal than skills alone.

Why $10 to unlock?+

It filters for serious people — someone who won't pay $10 isn't serious about building. Matching and rematching are always free; a credit is only spent when you unlock someone you actually want to contact. Not a fit? We keep searching — free.

Is my information private?+

Your contact is only shared after both sides unlock. We never sell data. Never share without permission.
